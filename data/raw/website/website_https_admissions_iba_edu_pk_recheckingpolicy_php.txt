Rechecking Policy
1- A written application for rechecking along with Pay-order of Rs. 5,000 in favor of IBA Karachi shall be submitted at Testing Department, IBA Main Campus
2- Rechecking fee will be refunded in case marks are increased.
3- Rechecking means:
a) Re-grading of MCQs answer sheet to check whether grading and total marks are correct.
b) Recounting of marks in case of Essay and Case Study to check whether they are correctly compiled and reported. Rechecking does not mean reassessment of Essay/Case Study script. Marks originally assigned by the examiner will           be considered final.
Note:
Based on the highly competitive process of result compilation, which involves three step verification check, the occurrence of any irregularity is extremely unlikely.
